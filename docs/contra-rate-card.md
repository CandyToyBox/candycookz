## Service Listing: Web3 Event Branding Package

**Title:** Web3 Event Branding Package

**Description:**

> End-to-end event branding for Web3 conferences, side events, hackathons, and launch parties. From logo design to signage to merch, you get a cohesive visual identity that makes your event look like it belongs at Art Basel, not a college meetup.
>
> I've designed and produced branding for events at Art Basel Miami and NFT NYC, handling everything from step-and-repeat banners to sponsorship decks to drink menus. I work fast when deadlines are tight and I know the Web3 space inside out, so there's no ramp-up time explaining what a hackathon is or why your mint party needs its own visual identity.
>
> You'll get a complete, print-ready and digital-ready brand package with a clear revision process and set deliverables, so you know exactly what you're getting and when.

**Core Deliverables:**

- **Logo & Brand Identity** — Custom event logo, color palette, typography, and brand guidelines document.
- **Print Materials** — Flyers, posters, banners, step-and-repeat backdrops, and signage files (print-ready).
- **Sponsorship Deck** — Polished pitch deck for securing sponsors (up to 15 slides).
- **Digital Assets** — Social media graphics, event page banners, email headers, and QR code designs.
- **Merch Design** — Designs for event merchandise (tees, hats, stickers, etc.) ready for Printify/Printful.
- **Event Collateral** — Drink menus, name badges, wristband designs, table cards, and on-site materials.

**Revisions:** 2 rounds per deliverable included (protects your time; extra rounds quoted separately).

## Founding-Client Move (to get your first reviews)

Your only real obstacle is zero reviews — not your price. Keep the listed price at $5,000. For your first 1–2 clients, offer a founding rate **in conversation**, not on the listing:

> "Since you'd be one of my first clients on Contra, I can do this at a founding rate of $3,000 instead of $5,000. In exchange, I'd ask for an honest review and permission to feature the work in my portfolio. After that the rate goes back to standard."

This gets you social proof fast without permanently underpricing yourself. Everyone after pays full freight.

## Income Math (target: $12,000/month)

After ~30% for taxes and overhead, you need to gross roughly **$17,000–$17,500/month** to net $12K.

- At 3 larger projects/month → ~$5,800 each
- At 5 projects/month → ~$3,500 each

**The real engine is retainers, not one-offs.** Event branding is lumpy and seasonal. Two or three retainer clients at $2,500–$4,000/month with clear deliverables is steadier than chasing five separate projects every month. Aim to convert happy project clients into retainers.

**Monthly overhead (~$170):** Claude ~$20, ChatGPT ~$20, Canva ~$15, Vercel ~$20, Supabase ~$25, fal.ai ~$20, Google Workspace+AI ~$30, X Premium ~$16.
